Due to the numerous outbreaks and recalls associated with Listeria, PMA Science & Technology Committee Listeria Task force developed this Listeria resources page to provide the produce industry with quick access to materials available on the internet regarding information about and control of Listeria monocytogenes in food facilities.  This resource page is intended to be an electronic file cabinet for produce industry professionals to assist them in understanding and implementing effective preventive control strategies to reduce, control and/or eliminate the risk of listeriosis in produce that they grow, pack, manufacture/process or hold.   


This resource list is intended to be living document that will be updated on regular basis whenever, new relevant information and resources related to the awareness, understanding, implementation of Listeria monocytogenes specific prevent controls and means of verifying Listeria monocytogenes preventive controls becomes available.

To allow users to peruse the available resources that may be of interest to them, resources documents are broadly categorized as follows: 

  • Government Risk Assessment, Guidance and Compliance Policy
  • Industry Guidance
  • Cooperative Extension Bulletins & Presentations
  • Trade Journal Publications
  • Scientific Publications
  • Training Courses
  • Blogs
Additionally, each hyperlink is tagged with one or more of four key words: Awareness, Understanding, Implementation, and/or Verification, to allow users to search for specific topics by simply using the word search function in their browser.  Simply hit Ctrl F; type in one of the four keywords (awareness, understanding, implementation or verification) and hit enter to quickly find resources of interest on this resources page. 

The keywords will help you quickly find the following types of information that you are specifically interested in:
  • Awareness Listeria monocytogenes contamination of food and listeriosis is important.
  • Understanding: resources related to the biology and facts regarding Listeria spp. and Listeria monocytogenes as well as U.S. and international regulatory requirements.
  • Implementation: resources related to the development and implementation of preventive control strategies for Listeria spp. and Listeria monocytogenes.
  • Verification: resources related to verification activities associated with Listeria spp. and Listeria monocytogenes preventive controls such as environmental monitoring programs and product or environmental testing

We hope this information is helpful and useful to you and if you are aware of any other resources that the produce industry should be directed to, please do forward them by email to Jim Gorny, Ph.D. Vice President of Food Safety & Technology at PMA so that we can post a link and make them available to everyone visiting this resource page.
