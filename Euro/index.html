United States Country Overview

Qualitative overview

  • Rise in consumer spending, robust growth of exports and acceleration in the business investment are the major drivers for the strong economic growth of the US.
  • House prices are on the rise due to a national construction slowdown, higher interest rates and booming city economies.
  • Individual tax cuts, high consumer confidence along with a tight labour market had resulted in a robust growth private consumption value in 2017.

Economic prospects

  • The USMCA trade deal, which replaces the NAFTA deal, was formally agreed on October 1, 2018. The USMCA gives the US greater access to Canada’s dairy industry, incentives, more domestic automobile production, as well as increases environmental and labour regulations and updates intellectual property legislation.
  • Farming is predominately large scale and generally efficient. Thus, even though the USA is a major exporter of foodstuffs and processed foods, agriculture accounts for only a tiny portion of GDP and employs just 1.4% of the workforce. Increased productivity is the main driver of agricultural growth.
  • The USA is a leader in the energy sector with high reserves of natural gas. Improvement in production technologies along with the US Shale Revolution is expected to help the USA overtake Russia as the world’s leading oil producer.

Fruit and Vegetable Market

Fruit analysis

  • Sales of fruits declined in total volume terms in 2017. The volatile weather caused massive disruption to the supply chain. Furthermore, the surge in fuel prices hindered the category growth.
  • Organic fruits are forecasted to have a bright future, mainly due to the increasing public awareness of organic trends, and larger investments being made by the government into subsidy programmes for farmers making the difficult transition to organic farming.
  • Fruits are influenced heavily by the public policy prioritisations and decisions by the federal government related to the regulations and budgetary funding. Often, contentious issues such as crop insurance subsidisation and food safety mechanisms have a measurable impact on the success of the US fruits industry.

Click here for more detailed information on fruits from Euromonitor

Vegetable analysis

  • Challenges stemming from the weather, workforce shortage and competing grocery items have resulted in vegetable sales witnessing negligible growth in total volume terms in 2017.
  • Recent consumer health trends have enabled cauliflowers and broccoli to witness the most dynamic growth amongst all vegetables in 2017. Both cauliflowers and broccoli benefit from nutritional values, high in fiber and low in carbohydrates.
  • Battles over issues like funding, regulations and crop insurance have always been staples of the US agriculture industry. However, additional issues such as droughts have further exacerbated an already challenging market.

Trends

  • Hypermarkets and internet retailing continued to gain market share within the grocery space by endeavouring to provide a wider selection of products.
  • Small grocers remained a key component of traditional grocery retailers in the US, especially in the larger US cities, where these stores – sometimes referred to as “bodegas” – are incredibly important to lower-income consumers.
  • Fixed-price stores have started to encroach upon traditional grocery retailers’ environment, with the leading chains such as the Dollar General and the Dollar Tree aggressively expanding their selection of groceries.

Click here for more detailed information on Retailing from Euromonitor

Prospects

  • Grocery retailers are incredibly important to lower income consumers and are thus more protected from the impact of internet retailing than most other retail channels in the US.
  • The future of SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) benefits continues to be clouded by the uncertainty due to certain political parties arguing for tightened eligibility requirements. – A situation that will continue to cast a shadow over the growth outlook for small grocers operating in the lower-income areas across the US.
  • The growing adoption of delivery services such as Instacart and Shipt is of growing concern to grocery retailers as these delivery services are better placed to neutralise the advantages of product selection.
